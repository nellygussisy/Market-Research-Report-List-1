<p><ul><li>Invasive Sub-acute Care Ventilators</li><li>Noninvasive Sub-acute Care Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sub-acute care ventilators market includes two primary types: invasive and noninvasive ventilators. Invasive sub-acute care ventilators involve the use of tubes inserted into the airway, providing mechanical support for patients who cannot breathe adequately on their own. In contrast, noninvasive sub-acute care ventilators deliver assistance through masks or similar devices, allowing patients to breathe spontaneously while receiving required airflow. Both types cater to patients with complex respiratory needs, facilitating recovery in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
