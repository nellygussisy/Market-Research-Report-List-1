<p><p>The on-platform veterinary ultrasound system market is divided into two main types: black and white ultrasound and color ultrasound. Black and white ultrasound, often referred to as grayscale ultrasound, provides clear images of tissues and structures, making it suitable for basic diagnostics. Color ultrasound, on the other hand, adds color flow imaging, allowing for enhanced evaluation of blood flow and movements within the body. Both types play crucial roles in veterinary practices, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
