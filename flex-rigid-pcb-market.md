<p><p>The Flex Rigid PCB market is categorized into three main types: single layer, double layer, and multi-layer. Single layer PCBs feature one conductive layer, making them cost-effective for simple applications. Double layer PCBs incorporate two conductive layers, offering enhanced functionality and design flexibility. Multi-layer PCBs, with three or more layers, support complex circuitry, enabling miniaturization and improved performance for advanced electronics. Each type serves specific demands across various industries, balancing cost, performance, and design requirements.</p></p>
