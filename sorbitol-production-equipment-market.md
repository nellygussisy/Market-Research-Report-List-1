<p><p>Sorbitol production equipment encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Hydrogenation Reactors** convert glucose to sorbitol through catalytic hydrogenation.</p><p>2. **Evaporators** remove excess water, concentrating the sorbitol solution.</p><p>3. **Ion Exchangers** purify the solution by removing impurities and enhancing quality.</p><p>4. **Sedimentation & Filtration Tanks** separate solid impurities from the liquid sorbitol, ensuring clarity. </p><p>5. **Others** may include auxiliary systems like heat exchangers and storage tanks, supporting the overall production process and enhancing efficiency.</p></p>
