<p><p>Air-cooled modular chillers are categorized into two main types: single-cooling and heat pump. Single-cooling type chillers primarily focus on cooling applications, utilizing refrigerant to absorb heat from indoor spaces and expel it outdoors. In contrast, heat pump type chillers can provide both cooling and heating by reversing the refrigeration cycle, making them versatile for year-round climate control. These modular systems offer scalability and efficiency, catering to various commercial and industrial needs while enhancing energy management.</p></p>
