<p><strong>The Centrifugal Pump for Pharmaceutical Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Suction Pumps</li><li>Double Suction Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the pharmaceutical market, centrifugal pumps are essential for transporting fluids efficiently. Single suction pumps draw fluid from one side, making them compact and cost-effective, suitable for low-flow applications. In contrast, double suction pumps have an inlet on both sides, balancing hydraulic forces, which reduces vibrations and enhances efficiency for high-capacity operations. Both types are critical in ensuring precise fluid transfer, maintaining sterility, and supporting various processes like formulation, mixing, and cleaning in pharmaceutical manufacturing environments.</p></p>
