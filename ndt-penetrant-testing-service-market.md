<p><strong>The NDT Penetrant Testing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immersion Method</li><li>Spray Method</li><li>Brush Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NDT penetrant testing service market comprises three primary methods: immersion, spray, and brush. The immersion method involves submerging parts in a penetrant liquid, ensuring even coverage and deep penetration for defect detection. The spray method utilizes aerosolized penetrant applied directly to the surface, ideal for larger or more complex geometries. Lastly, the brush method allows for manual application of the penetrant, giving operators control over areas requiring focused examination. Each method serves distinct requirements in non-destructive testing applications.</p></p>
