<p><strong>The Photosensitive Dielectric Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Tone Type</li><li>Negative Tone Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive dielectric material market is categorized into two main types: positive tone and negative tone. Positive tone materials become soluble upon exposure to light, allowing selective removal of exposed areas to create patterns. Conversely, negative tone materials harden when exposed, making the unexposed areas removable. These materials are crucial in various applications, such as photolithography for electronics and microfabrication, where precise patterning is essential for performance and functionality in semiconductor and related industries.</p></p>
