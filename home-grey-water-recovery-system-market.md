<p><strong>The Home Grey Water Recovery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Gravity-Based Systems"</li><li>"Pumped Systems"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Home Grey Water Recovery System market can be categorized into two main types: Gravity-Based Systems and Pumped Systems. Gravity-Based Systems rely on natural gravitational flow to transport grey water from sinks, showers, and washing machines to storage for reuse, making them low-maintenance and energy-efficient. In contrast, Pumped Systems actively use pumps to move grey water, allowing for greater flexibility in installation, especially in homes where gravity flow is insufficient. Both systems aim to conserve water and promote sustainability.</p></p>
