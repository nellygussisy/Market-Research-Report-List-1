<p><p>The organic biological fertilizer market encompasses several key types. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers enhance soil nitrogen levels by utilizing specific bacteria, promoting plant growth. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers help release insoluble phosphates in the soil, making them more accessible to plants. Potash-mobilizing fertilizers increase potassium availability, which is vital for various plant functions. Other types may include microbial inoculants and organic matter-enhancing products. Together, these fertilizers contribute to sustainable agriculture by improving soil health and nutrient availability.</p></p>
