<p><p>Vertical Surge Tanks (VST) are essential in hydraulic systems to manage pressure fluctuations and maintain system stability. The market comprises two main types: Single Compartment and Twin Compartment Surge Tanks. Single Compartment VSTs are designed for straightforward applications, offering efficiency and simplicity. In contrast, Twin Compartment VSTs provide enhanced performance by allowing for better pressure regulation and isolation of hydraulic flows, making them suitable for more complex systems requiring advanced pressure management and operational reliability.</p></p>
