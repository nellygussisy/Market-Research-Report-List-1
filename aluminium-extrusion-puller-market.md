<p><p>The aluminium extrusion puller market is segmented into two main types: aluminium extrusion double pullers and aluminium extrusion single pullers. Double pullers efficiently manage two profiles simultaneously, increasing production capacity and reducing cycle time, making them suitable for high-volume operations. In contrast, single pullers are designed for handling one profile at a time, offering greater precision and flexibility, ideal for custom or low-volume production. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs within the aluminium extrusion industry.</p></p>
