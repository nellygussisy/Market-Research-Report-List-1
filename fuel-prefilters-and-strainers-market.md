<p><p>The fuel prefilters and strainers market is divided into two main types: fuel prefilter systems and fuel strainers. Fuel prefilters are designed to remove particulates and contaminants from fuel before it reaches the main filtration system, enhancing engine performance and longevity. Conversely, fuel strainers serve a similar purpose but focus on larger particles, typically placed at the inlet of the fuel system. Both types are essential for maintaining fuel quality and preventing system damage in various applications, including automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
