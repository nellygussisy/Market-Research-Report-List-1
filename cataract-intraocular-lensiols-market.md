<p><p>The cataract intraocular lens (IOL) market primarily features monofocal and multifocal lenses. Monofocal IOLs provide a single focus point, typically correcting distance vision, requiring patients to use glasses for near tasks. In contrast, multifocal IOLs offer multiple focal points, allowing for improved vision at varying distances without the need for glasses. This differentiation caters to patients with diverse visual needs post-cataract surgery, influencing market demand and innovation in lens design for enhanced patient outcomes.</p></p>
