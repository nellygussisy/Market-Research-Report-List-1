<p><strong>The Manual Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Control Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual valve market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions. Gate valves are designed for on/off control, providing minimal flow resistance. Control valves regulate flow and pressure in systems, allowing for precise adjustments. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities with a spherical disc design, ensuring robust sealing. Butterfly valves are lightweight and efficient, using a rotating disc to manage flow with minimal pressure drop. Together, these valves cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring effective fluid management in processes.</p></p>
