<p><p>The organic celery juice market is primarily divided into two types: cold-pressed celery juice and conventional celery juice. Cold-pressed celery juice is produced through a method that preserves nutrients by using hydraulic pressure, resulting in a fresher taste and higher nutrient retention. In contrast, conventional celery juice refers to juice made from mechanically processed celery, which may undergo pasteurization, leading to a longer shelf life but potentially less nutritional value. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and health-conscious choices.</p></p>
