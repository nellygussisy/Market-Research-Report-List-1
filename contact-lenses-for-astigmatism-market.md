<p><p>The contact lenses for astigmatism market is categorized into two main types: conventional hydrogel material and highly breathable silicone hydrogel. Conventional hydrogel lenses offer basic comfort and moisture retention, suitable for mild astigmatism but may lack durability. In contrast, highly breathable silicone hydrogel lenses allow more oxygen to reach the cornea, enhancing comfort and minimizing dryness. These lenses are ideal for individuals with higher prescriptions and those seeking extended wear options, as they provide superior visual clarity and eye health.</p></p>
