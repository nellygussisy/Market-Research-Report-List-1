<p><p>The plant hemicellulose market is categorized into three main types: nonionic, anionic, and cationic hemicellulose. Nonionic hemicellulose is neutral and provides structural support without altering pH. Anionic hemicellulose carries a negative charge, enhancing water retention and stability in formulations. Cationic hemicellulose, on the other hand, has a positive charge, offering improved adhesion properties and compatibility with various substances. Each type serves specialized applications in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture, driven by their unique chemical properties and functionalities.</p></p>
