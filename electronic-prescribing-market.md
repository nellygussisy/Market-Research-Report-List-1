<p><ul><li>Integrated Systems</li><li>Stand-alone Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic prescribing market is categorized into integrated systems and stand-alone systems. Integrated systems are fully incorporated within electronic health record (EHR) platforms, allowing seamless data exchange and enhancing clinical workflows. These systems improve efficiency and reduce errors by centralizing patient information and prescriptions. Stand-alone systems, on the other hand, operate independently of EHRs, focusing solely on prescribing functions. They may be preferred by smaller practices seeking cost-effective solutions without the complexity of integrated platforms, but can lack comprehensive patient data access.</p></p>
