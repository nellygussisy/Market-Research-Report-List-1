<p><strong>The Mortar Plasticiser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powdery</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mortar plasticiser market is divided into two main types: powdery and liquid. Powdery plasticisers are typically mixed with dry materials before water is added, enhancing workability and consistency in dry blends. Liquid plasticisers, on the other hand, are added during the mixing process, providing immediate effects on adhesion and flowability. Both types improve the performance of mortar, reduce water content, and enhance durability, making them essential for various construction applications. Their choice depends on project specifications and application methods.</p></p>
