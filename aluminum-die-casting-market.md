<p><strong>The Aluminum Die Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Die Casting</li><li>High Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum die casting market comprises several methods, primarily categorized into low pressure die casting and high pressure die casting. Low pressure die casting involves pouring molten aluminum into a die under controlled pressure, resulting in high-quality parts with excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy. High pressure die casting, on the other hand, injects molten aluminum into the die at high pressure, enabling faster production rates and complex geometries. Other methods may include vacuum die casting and permanent mold casting, each serving specific applications.</p></p>
