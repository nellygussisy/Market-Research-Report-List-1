<p><ul><li>Pure Electric Power</li><li>Hybrid Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light-duty electric truck market is divided into two primary types: Pure Electric Power and Hybrid Power. Pure electric trucks operate solely on electric batteries, offering zero emissions and lower operational costs, making them ideal for urban deliveries. In contrast, hybrid power trucks combine an internal combustion engine with electric propulsion, providing flexibility for longer ranges and reduced fuel consumption. This hybrid approach caters to users needing both electric efficiency and the ability to travel longer distances without charging infrastructure dependence.</p></p>
