<p><p>The Industrial and Commercial Desiccant Dehumidifier market is primarily categorized into Tower Type and Rotor Type systems. Tower Type dehumidifiers utilize vertical columns filled with desiccant materials to absorb moisture from the air, ideal for large-scale applications needing low humidity levels. Rotor Type systems, on the other hand, employ rotating wheels coated with desiccant substances, continuously taking in and drying air. Both types effectively control humidity, improving air quality and preventing mold growth in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
