<p><ul><li>Bearing Type</li><li>Stator Embedded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high voltage motor temperature sensor market consists of two primary types: bearing type and stator embedded type. Bearing type sensors monitor the temperature of motor bearings to ensure optimal performance and prevent overheating, thereby extending the motor's lifespan. Stator embedded type sensors are integrated directly into the motor's stator, providing real-time temperature data for more precise thermal management. Both types enhance motor reliability and efficiency, catering to various industrial applications requiring robust temperature monitoring solutions.</p></p>
