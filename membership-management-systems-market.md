<p><p>Membership Management Systems (MMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises systems are installed locally on an organization's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring substantial IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted online, providing accessibility from anywhere and automatic updates, which reduces IT burdens. Organizations often choose based on their specific needs, budget, and resources, balancing control with convenience in managing member information and engagement.</p></p>
