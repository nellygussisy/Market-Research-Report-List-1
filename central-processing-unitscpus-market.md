<p><p>Central Processing Units (CPUs) are essential components of computers, responsible for executing instructions and processing data. The market for CPUs encompasses various packaging technologies, including Ball Grid Array (BGA) and Chip-on-Substrate (CSP). BGA integrates the chip directly onto the substrate, enhancing performance and thermal management, ideal for compact devices. CSP technology, on the other hand, involves mounting chips on substrates, minimizing size and weight while optimizing power efficiency. These packaging methods reflect the industry's need for improved performance in diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
