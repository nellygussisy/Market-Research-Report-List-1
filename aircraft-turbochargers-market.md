<p><ul><li>Butterfly Valve Type</li><li>Poppet Valve Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft turbocharger market is primarily segmented into two types: butterfly valve type and poppet valve type. The butterfly valve type features a flat circular disc that pivots to control airflow, offering efficient pressure adjustments and rapid response. In contrast, the poppet valve type utilizes a spring-loaded mechanism to open and close the valve, providing reliable sealing and consistent performance. Both types are essential for enhancing engine efficiency and power output in various aircraft applications, catering to different operational requirements.</p></p>
