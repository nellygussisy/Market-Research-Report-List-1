<p><p>The Multiple Listing Service (MLS) software market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based MLS software offers users remote access and scalability, allowing real estate professionals to manage listings from any location with an internet connection, ensuring real-time updates and collaboration. In contrast, on-premises MLS software is installed locally on a user's servers, providing enhanced control over data security and customization, but may require significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. Each type serves different operational needs and preferences within the real estate industry.</p></p>
