<p><p>The Ambulatory Patient Portal market is typically divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based portals are hosted on remote servers, offering accessibility from anywhere with internet connectivity, facilitating easier updates and scalability. In contrast, on-premise portals are installed directly on the healthcare provider's servers, providing more control over data security and customization but may involve higher maintenance costs and limited accessibility. Both types aim to enhance patient engagement and streamline communication in outpatient settings.</p></p>
