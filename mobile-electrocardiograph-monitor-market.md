<p><p>The Mobile Electrocardiograph Monitor market encompasses various measurement types, including thoracometry, legometry, and hand measurement. Thoracometry involves assessing heart function through measurements taken from the chest area, providing insights into cardiac activity. Legometry focuses on readings obtained from the lower limbs, often useful for monitoring peripheral circulation and related cardiac functions. Hand measurement involves capturing ECG data from the hands, offering a convenient and portable solution for cardiac assessments. Together, these methods enhance patient care by enabling versatile, real-time monitoring.</p></p>
