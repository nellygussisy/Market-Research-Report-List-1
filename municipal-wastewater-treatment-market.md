<p><p>The municipal wastewater treatment market encompasses various technologies for effective water purification. Reverse osmosis membranes remove contaminants by forcing water through a semipermeable membrane. Ion-exchange processes replace undesirable ions with less harmful ones. Ultrafiltration (UF) membranes filter out particles and microorganisms. Electrodeionization (EDI) combines ion-exchange and electrochemical processes for deionization. Biological filtration utilizes microorganisms to break down pollutants, while chemical conditioning involves adding substances to enhance treatment. Other methods may include advanced oxidation and other proprietary technologies to improve wastewater quality.</p></p>
