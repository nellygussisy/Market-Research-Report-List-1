Overhead line fault indicators are designed for overhead power lines, providing real-time information on faults to minimize outage times. Cable fault indicators are used for underground cables, detecting insulation failures or conductive faults. Panel fault indicators monitor electrical panels, alerting users to issues within circuitry. Other types encompass specialized fault detection devices tailored for unique applications, enhancing reliability and safety across electrical infrastructures.</p></p>
