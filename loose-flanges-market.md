<p><strong>The Loose Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF</li><li>FM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Loose flanges are utilized in various industrial applications to facilitate the assembly and disassembly of piping systems. The RF (Raised Face) type features a small raised area on the flange face, providing a tighter seal when bolted to a corresponding flange. The FM (Flat Mask) type, on the other hand, has a flat surface that allows for the easy installation and alignment of components. Both flange types play crucial roles in ensuring leak-free connections while accommodating varying pressures and temperatures in different market sectors.</p></p>
